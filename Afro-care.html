Afro Care ✧

← Back to Hair

Step 1: Pre-Wash Prep ✧

Start by detangling gently with your fingers before washing. Optional: Apply a light pre-poo (like coconut or olive oil) to protect hair from shampoo dryness.

Step 2: Shampoo ✧

Drench hair with water, then apply a gentle shampoo.
Dry hair? Choose a moisturizing shampoo.
Dandruff? Use a shampoo with tea tree or zinc pyrithione.
Rinse thoroughly.

Step 3: Conditioner ✧

Separate hair into 4–6 sections. Apply conditioner to each section. Use your fingers to distribute, then detangle gently with a wide-tooth brush. Trim any stray hairs or split ends.

Step 4: Rinse & Moisturize ✧

Wash out the conditioner gently. Pat hair dry with a microfiber towel or old t-shirt to reduce frizz. Apply leave-in conditioner or moisturizer to damp hair to lock in hydration.

Step 5: Style ✧

Style your afro however you like—twist-outs, buns, or free afros. Make sure hair is fully moisturized and protected with a satin scarf or bonnet at night to maintain softness and reduce breakage.
