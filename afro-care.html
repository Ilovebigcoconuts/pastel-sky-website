Afro Hair Care ✧彡

A full gentle wash day routine to keep your afro healthy, moisturized, and thriving ♡

1. Pre-Poo ✧

Start with dry hair. Apply an oil of your choice (amla, olive oil, coconut oil, etc). Focus on the ends + gently detangle with fingers before adding water.

2. Shampoo ✧

Fully drench your hair in warm water. Use a shampoo depending on your needs:
• Dry hair → moisturizing shampoo
• Itchy scalp/dandruff → clarifying or dandruff shampoo
• Product buildup → sulfate clarifying (only sometimes)

3. Condition & Detangle ✧

Section your hair into 4–6 parts. Apply conditioner generously to each section. First finger-detangle, then use a detangling brush.

Trim any hanging broken ends. You have split ends if: • ends look frizzy or uneven • strands split into 2–3 tiny pieces

4. Moisturize ✧

Apply a leave-in or hair lotion (like the one shown). Follow with an oil to seal everything in.

5. Style ✧

Once moisturized, style however you like — puffs, twists, buns, etc. Just make sure hair stays moisturized and protected ♡
