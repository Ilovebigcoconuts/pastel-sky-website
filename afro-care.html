Afro Hair Care Guide ✧

Step 1: Pre-Poo ✧

Start on dry hair and apply an oil of your choice — amla oil, coconut oil, olive oil, grapeseed oil, or any blend you like. This softens tangles, reduces breakage, and protects your hair during shampooing. Finger-detangle gently and leave it in for 20–40 minutes.

Step 2: Shampoo ✧

Make sure your hair is fully drenched with warm water. Use a shampoo that matches your hair’s needs: • If your hair feels dry → a moisturizing shampoo • If you have buildup/dandruff → a clarifying or anti-dandruff shampoo Massage your scalp gently using your fingertips — not nails — then rinse.

Step 3: Conditioner ✧

Split your hair into 4–6 sections. Apply conditioner to each section and work it through with your fingers first. Then use a detangling brush, starting at the ends and working upward. Trim any knots or broken ends hanging off — this reduces tangles next wash day.

Step 4: Moisturize ✧

After rinsing out the conditioner, apply a moisturizer, curl cream, or hair lotion. Focus on your ends first since they dry the quickest. Make sure every section is coated so your hair stays hydrated and soft all week.

Trim any hanging broken ends. You have split ends if: • ends look frizzy or uneven • strands split into 2–3 tiny pieces

5. Style ✧

Once moisturized, style however you like — puffs, twists, buns, etc. Just make sure hair stays moisturized and protected ♡
