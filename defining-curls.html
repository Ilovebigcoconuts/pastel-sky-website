Defining Your Curls ✧

← Back to Hair

Step 1: Start on Clean Hair ✧

Make sure your hair is freshly washed and still wet. This helps curls form better and stay defined.

Step 2: Divide into Sections ✧

Separate hair into small, manageable sections. This ensures every curl gets attention and product.

Step 3: Apply Leave-In & Curl Definer ✧

On each section, add a leave-in conditioner and then your favorite curl-defining cream or gel.

Step 4: Detangle & Clump Curls ✧

Use a Denman brush or your fingers to separate and clump each curl. Pull down gently to help curls form naturally.

Step 5: Dry & Set ✧

Let your curls air dry or use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment. Avoid touching hair too much while drying to prevent frizz.

Extra Tip ✧

For extra bounce, scrunch your curls upward as they dry. Use a satin scarf or bonnet at night to maintain curl definition.
